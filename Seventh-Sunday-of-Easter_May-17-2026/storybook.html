After Jesus rose from the dead, he spent time with his disciples for forty days, teaching them and eating with them. One day, they all gathered on a hilltop called the Mount of Olives. The disciples had a big question. They asked Jesus: "Lord, is this the time when you will finally make everything right for Israel?"
* Can you lean in and look really curious, like you are about to ask someone a big question?
Jesus looked at his friends and said: "It is not for you to know when. That is up to God. But here is what I can tell you: you will receive power when the Holy Spirit comes to you. And you will be my witnesses in Jerusalem, in all of Judea, in Samaria, and all the way to the ends of the earth."
* Cup your hands around your mouth like a megaphone. You are a witness! Tell the person next to you: "To the ends of the earth!"
And then, right before their eyes, Jesus was lifted up into the sky. A cloud came and took him out of their sight. The disciples stood there staring up at the sky, amazed. Then two men in bright white robes appeared and said: "Why are you standing here looking up? Jesus will come back the same way you saw him go."
* Look up at the ceiling. Shield your eyes with your hand like you are staring into bright light.
So the disciples walked back to Jerusalem and went to the upper room where they were staying. Peter, John, James, Andrew, and all the others. The women were there too, including Mary, Jesus' mother. And together, they did exactly what they knew how to do. They prayed. They waited. And they trusted that what Jesus promised would come true.
* Close your eyes, fold your hands, and take one deep breath. That is what waiting and trusting looks like.
